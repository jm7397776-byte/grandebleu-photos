## Deciphering the Tour Options and Price Points for 2026

Grande Bleu offers two distinct tours, each promising its own magic. Understanding the pricing is key to planning your perfect romantic outing. Here’s a breakdown of the 2026 rates:

*   **Luxury Tour:** This daytime journey offers pristine views of the coastline. The adult regular price is ₩60,000, and for children, it's ₩40,000. Keep an eye out for promotional rates, which can bring the adult fare down to ₩48,000 and the child fare to ₩28,000.

*   **Sunset Tour:** For those yearning for a more romantic ambiance, the Sunset Tour paints the sky with fiery hues. The adult regular price stands at ₩70,000, and children at ₩40,000. Promotional rates make it even more enticing, with adult tickets available for ₩56,000 and child tickets for ₩28,000.

These rates offer couples the flexibility to choose an experience that aligns with their budget and romantic vision. You can book your tour through various partner platforms like Naver, Klook, KKday, and Ctrip/Trip.com, making it incredibly convenient to secure your spot.

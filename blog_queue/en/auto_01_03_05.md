## Deciding on Your Perfect Tour

Grande Bleu Yacht offers two primary options perfect for families: the Luxury Tour and the Sunset Tour. The Luxury Tour, lasting an hour, allows you to soak in the daytime beauty of the Daepo coastline, perfect for snapping vibrant photos of Wolpyeong Jusangjeolli and the Wolpyeong Sea Cave. If you're looking for that magical golden hour experience, the Sunset Tour is equally fantastic, painting the sky with incredible hues. Both tours allow for 91 guests across two yachts (Yacht 550 seats 44 and Yacht 620 seats 47), ensuring a spacious and comfortable journey for everyone.

For the Luxury Tour, the adult price is ₩60,000 and child ₩40,000, with a promotional price of ₩48,000 for adults and ₩28,000 for children. The Sunset Tour is priced at ₩70,000 for adults and ₩40,000 for children, with promo rates of ₩56,000 for adults and ₩28,000 for children. These options cater well to different family schedules and preferences.

## How to Book Your Family's Grand Adventure

Booking our spots on the Grande Bleu Yacht was surprisingly straightforward, which is a huge plus when traveling with kids. Grande Bleu Yacht partners with several popular online platforms, making it accessible for international and domestic travelers alike. You can easily find and book your tour through Naver, Klook, KKday, or Ctrip/Trip.com partner platforms. We personally found the process on one of these platforms to be intuitive, allowing us to select our preferred tour time and date with just a few clicks. It's always a good idea to book in advance, especially during the peak summer months of June 2026, to secure your desired slot.

Should you have any specific questions or need assistance, their contact number is +82-64-739-7776, and their Instagram handle is @gb.jeju, where you can often find stunning visuals and updates.
